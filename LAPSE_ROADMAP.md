# Lapse

## Vision

Lapse is a social exploration platform where people discover the world through real people, authentic communities, photos, videos, and conversations.

No fake engagement.
No fake online users.
No fake notifications.

Everything shown in Lapse should represent real activity.

## Principles

- Real people first
- Beautiful but simple
- Fast on mobile
- Privacy respected
- Communities over follower counts
- Explore before chat
- Quality over quantity

## MVP

- Explore
- Communities
- Chat
- Profile
- Settings

## Future

- Events
